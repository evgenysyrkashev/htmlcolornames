HTMLColorNames. Salmon, chocolate or maybe tomato?

There are three common way to define a color in HTML: RGB, HEX and color name. Last one is most interesting. You can use a word to describe a color in HTML. Unfortunately you can't use whatever word you want. PuppyPaws doesn't work. But there is a list of allowed html color names and all of them you can find on HTMLColorNames.

Did you notice that Magenta and Fuchsia are absolutely the same as well as Aqua and Cyan?

American english or British english? There are no difference between Gray and Grey. Both of them give you the same color.
